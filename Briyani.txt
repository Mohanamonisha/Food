==============
chickenbriyani
==============
Chicken biryani in a pressure cooker all most sounds impossible.
 Many people are sceptical of cooking rice in pressure cooker let alone a biryani. 
 But trust me if you follow the soaking process of rice and use the right quantity you will be able to prepare beautiful delicious chicken biryani in your own kitchen. 
 This biryani recipe is requires less effort yet the results are awesome. 
 This chicken biryani can be prepared for your next Dinner Party or potluck. 
 The beauty of this chicken biryani in pressure cooker is the use of freshly grounded biryani masala. 
 It goes well with onion-tomato raita and some kebabs.
