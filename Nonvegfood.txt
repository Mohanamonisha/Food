The Non-Vegetarian side of Indian cuisine comprises of many juicy, tender delicacies made with eggs, mutton, chicken, fish etc. 
There is a great variety of meat, poultry and fish dishes in Indian Cuisine, here we have made an effort to present most of the non-vegetarian dishes served as snacks, accompaniments and main dishes like tandoori tikkas, kababs, roshan goshts, butter chicken, biryani and much more.
