Indian food is different from rest of the world not only in taste but also in cooking methods. 
It reflects a perfect blend of various cultures and ages. Just like Indian culture, food in India has also been influenced by various civilizations, 
Which have contributed their share in its overall development and the present form.

sweets

(Traditional Indian Sweet & Desserts) In most cultures, dessert is the final course of a meal that is usually a sweet dish or drink. 
In India, sweets are an important part of all festivals, functions or any happy occasion. 
Most Indian desserts include milk or ghee as its main ingredient. 
Milk sweets occupy the first place in all Indian bakeries.
